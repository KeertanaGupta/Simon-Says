Simon Says Game

"It's a Memory Game"

Instructions:

  1. Start the Game:
    • Press any key or tap the screen to begin.
  2. Repeat the Colors:
    • Remember the sequence of colors shown.
  3. Click or Tap:
    • Click or tap the colors in the same order.
  4. Mistakes:
    • A wrong click ends the game. Watch out for red flashes and a "wrong" sound.
  5. Restart:
    • After a mistake, press any key or tap to start a new game.
  6. Sound:
    • Listen for color cues. Each color has a unique sound.
  7. Have Fun:
    • Enjoy the challenge and have fun!
